# Requirements: Milestone 1

## Goal
Prepare high-quality sales documentation for two distinct markets.

## Must-Haves
- Professional tone and formatting.
- Clear value proposition for each market.
- Technical overview of the system.
- Visual/Design system highlights.

## USA Client Pitch Requirements
- Focus on scalability.
- Emphasis on premium "Industrial Elegance" UI/UX.
- Detailed financial analytics and reporting.
- Compliance and international standards.

## Mymensingh Local Client Pitch Requirements
- Focus on ROI and cost-savings.
- Emphasis on inventory management and stock tracking.
- Local market context (Bangladeshi restaurant operations).
- Ease of use for staff and owners.
- Integration with local systems/needs.
