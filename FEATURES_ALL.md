# Craving: Comprehensive Feature List

Craving is an all-in-one, modern Restaurant Management System (RMS) designed to bridge the gap between high-end dining experiences and robust operational efficiency.

## 1. Professional POS (Point of Sale) System
The core of the restaurant's daily operations, optimized for speed and precision.
- **Dynamic Billing**: Rapid item selection with real-time subtotal/total calculation.
- **Payment Flexibility**:
  - **Multi-Method Support**: Accept Cash, Card, and MFS (Mobile Financial Services like bKash, Nagad).
  - **Split Payments**: Allow customers to split a single bill across different payment methods.
- **Order States**:
  - **Hold/Retrieve**: Pause active orders for later processing without losing data.
  - **Served Orders**: Track and manage orders that are currently being served.
- **Kitchen & Bill Printing**:
  - **KOT (Kitchen Order Ticket)**: Automated thermal printing for kitchen staff.
  - **Professional Receipts**: High-quality bill printing with QR codes for digital verification.
- **Table Integration**: Visual representation of table statuses (Free, Occupied, Booked).
- **Add-on Logic**: Seamlessly add optional extras (e.g., extra cheese, toppings) to any item.
- **Financial Controls**: Apply percentage or fixed discounts and automated VAT calculations.

## 2. Customer-Facing Features
A premium digital storefront to engage and serve customers directly.
- **Online Ordering**: Full digital menu with categories, item details, and images.
- **Real-Time Order Tracking**: Step-by-step tracking from "Order Placed" to "Delivered".
- **Advanced Reservations**: Integrated table booking system with date and time selection.
- **Shopping Cart**: Intuitive cart management with add-on support.
- **Responsive Interface**: Fully optimized for smartphones, tablets, and desktops.
- **Content Hub**: Integrated Blog and Event sections for marketing and updates.

## 3. Administrative & Management Tools
Comprehensive control over the business from a centralized dashboard.
- **Advanced Dashboard**: HUD-style analytics for Sales, Profit/Loss, and Net Profit.
- **Inventory Management**: 
  - Track stock levels for all menu items and categories.
  - Manage stock-in/stock-out and wastage.
- **Staff Management**: 
  - Manage staff profiles, roles, and contact information.
  - Role-based access control (e.g., DSR role safeguards).
- **CRM (Customer Relationship Management)**: Maintain a database of customers with order history.
- **Delivery Management**: 
  - Assign orders to delivery personnel.
  - Track delivery status in real-time.
- **Table Management**: Configure restaurant layout, table numbers, and capacities.

## 4. Technical & Backend Capabilities
Robust infrastructure ensuring system reliability and scalability.
- **Automated SMS Notifications**: Integrated with MimSMS for instant order confirmations.
- **Role-Based Security**: Granular permissions for different staff levels.
- **Modern Tech Stack**: Built with React, TypeScript, Vite, and Node.js for high performance.
- **Industrial Elegance UI**: A premium design system optimized for low-light restaurant environments.
- **Data Integrity**: Full audit trails for returns, refunds, and cancellations.

## 5. Reporting & Insights
Data-driven decision-making tools.
- **Daily Sales Reports**: Detailed breakdown of daily revenue.
- **Item Performance**: Identify top-selling items and categories.
- **Financial Reconciliation**: Sync profit calculations across various dashboard metrics.
